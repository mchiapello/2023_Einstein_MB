Instructor

My name is Marco Chiapello ans I am currently working as Application Engineer at Agilent Technologies in Diagnostics & Genomics Group. My daily focus is on secondary analysis and customer care. I am a big supporter of reproducible and open research. My main tools are R, git, vim and docker. I am a curious person and a dedicated worker, I love the challenges of computational biology and teaching the principle of data analysis and visualization.

Teaching Philosophy

I have 3 goals:
- Motivate students to study.
- Increase the use of effective study and teaching strategies that are backed by research.
- Decrease negative views of testing.

Based on The Learning Scientists.

Code of Conduct

I am dedicated to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all people, regardless of background or identity. By participating in this class, participants accept to abide by teacher’ Code of Conduct and accept the procedures by which any Code of Conduct incidents are resolved. Any form of behaviour to exclude, intimidate, or cause discomfort is a violation of the Code of Conduct. In order to foster a positive and professional learning environment I encourage the following kinds of behaviours in all platforms and events:

  • Use welcoming and inclusive language
  • Be respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
  • Gracefully accept constructive criticism
  • Focus on what is best for the community
  • Show courtesy and respect towards other community members
